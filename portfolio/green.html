Verdant Constant

Date: January 29, 2026

My love is the listless feeling of not really waking up until your "good morning" lights the dark corners beside my bed
My love is the moss on the riverbank, the glass worn smooth by an emerald sea
My love is the presence of years, building worlds one letter at a time, until we can't remember what a day was like without you
My love is leaves and vines and stems and sprouts, rooted without promise where flowers never bloom

My love is the love that cannot be named, for if I say "I love you,"
The world will see red where there was only green.

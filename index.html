About

Hey there! My name is Alexia Ceballos. I'm a writer and editor specializing in narrative nonfiction and short fiction. My focus is on identity, lived experience, and the systems that shape people's lives. My work blends rigorous research with voice-driven story telling, translating complex and deeply personal subjects into clear, engaging narratives.

I write reported features, essays, and fiction, and edit such works for voice, structure, and clarity. My editorial works emphasizes narrative cohesion and audience engacement, with experience collaborating across design and teams with Google Workspace, Adobe Suite, and web development tools.
